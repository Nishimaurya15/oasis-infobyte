Hi! I'm RATAN TATA

PHILANTHROPIST

Mr Ratan N Tata was the Chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company
of the Tata group, from 1991 till his retirement on December 28, 2012.
Tata Group donated Rs. 950 million to the Indian Institute of Technology,
Bombay in 2014 and formed Tata Center for Technology and Design (TCTD).
It was the largest ever donation received in the history of the institute.
